Top 5 Grossing Movies of Johnny Depp of All times

Technical Wajed
12th Mar 2023
Next Blog

Youtube Video: 

Johnny Depp is a famous American actor who has appeared in numerous Hollywood movies. Here are some of his most notable roles and films:

Edward Scissorhands (1990)

What’s Eating Gilbert Grape (1993)

Donnie Brasco (1997)

Sleepy Hollow (1999)

Pirates of the Caribbean franchise (2003-2017)

Finding Neverland (2004)

Charlie and the Chocolate Factory (2005)

Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street (2007)

Public Enemies (2009)

Alice in Wonderland (2010)

The Lone Ranger (2013)

Black Mass (2015)

Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (2016)

Murder on the Orient Express (2017)

Richard Says Goodbye (2018)

These are just a few of his many movies, as Johnny Depp has had a long and successful career in Hollywood.

5. Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (2016) – $814M

Johnny Depp played the role of Gellert Grindelwald in the Fantastic Beasts film series, including the first film, Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them 2016. As for the gross earnings of the film, it grossed over $814 million worldwide, regardless of Johnny Depp’s involvement.

“Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them” is a 2016 fantasy film directed by David Yates and written by J.K. Rowling. The film is a spin-off and prequel to the Harry Potter film series, and it follows the adventures of Newt Scamander in New York City’s secret community of witches and wizards seventy years before Harry Potter reads his book in school.

The film was a commercial success, grossing $814 million at the worldwide box office. It received positive reviews from critics and audiences alike, with praise going to the film’s visuals, performances, and world-building.

The film starred Eddie Redmayne as Newt Scamander, Katherine Waterston as Porpentina Goldstein, Dan Fogler as Jacob Kowalski, Alison Sudol as Queenie Goldstein, and Colin Farrell as Percival Graves.

A sequel, “Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald,” was released in 2018, and a third film, “Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore,” is currently in production and set to be released in April 2022.

4. Pirates of the Caribbean: At World’s End (2007) $961M

“Pirates of the Caribbean: At World’s End” is a 2007 American fantasy swashbuckler film directed by Gore Verbinski and produced by Jerry Bruckheimer. The film is the third installment in the “Pirates of the Caribbean” film series and features an ensemble cast including Johnny Depp, Orlando Bloom, Keira Knightley, Geoffrey Rush, and Bill Nighy.
The movie was released on May 25, 2007, and grossed $961 million worldwide, making it the third highest-grossing film of 2007 and the eighth highest-grossing film of all time at the time of its release. The film was generally well-received by audiences and critics, with praise given to the performances, special effects, and action sequences.
The plot of the movie revolves around Captain Jack Sparrow, who is trapped in Davy Jones’ Locker and must find a way to escape and unite with his allies to fight against the East India Trading Company, which has taken control of the seas. The film also explores the relationship between Will Turner, Elizabeth Swann, and Captain Barbossa, as they struggle to prevent their own personal agendas from interfering with the mission to save Jack Sparrow.
Overall, “Pirates of the Caribbean: At World’s End” was a box office success and a critical hit, solidifying the “Pirates of the Caribbean” franchise as a blockbuster series.

3. Alice in Wonderland (2010) $1.02B

“Alice in Wonderland” is a 2010 fantasy film directed by Tim Burton, and starring Mia Wasikowska, Johnny Depp, and Helena Bonham Carter. The film was produced by Walt Disney Pictures and released on March 5, 2010.

The movie grossed over $1.02 billion worldwide, making it one of the highest-grossing films of all time.

2. Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides (2011) $1.04B

“Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides” is a 2011 adventure fantasy film directed by Rob Marshall and starring Johnny Depp, Penlope Cruz, and Ian McShane. The film is the fourth installment in the “Pirates of the Caribbean” film series and was released on May 20, 2011.

The film was a commercial success, grossing over $1.04 billion worldwide. It is currently the 42nd highest-grossing film of all time.

1. Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man’s Chest (2006) $1.06B

“Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man’s Chest” is a 2006 American fantasy swashbuckler film directed by Gore Verbinski and produced by Jerry Bruckheimer. The film was a huge commercial success, grossing $1.06 billion worldwide.

The movie is the second installment in the “Pirates of the Caribbean” film series, following the 2003 film “Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl” and preceding the 2007 film “Pirates of the Caribbean: At World’s End.” It stars Johnny Depp as Captain Jack Sparrow, Orlando Bloom as Will Turner, and Keira Knightley as Elizabeth Swann.

The plot of “Dead Man’s Chest” revolves around Captain Jack Sparrow’s attempt to avoid a debt he owes to the legendary Davy Jones, captain of the ghostly Flying Dutchman. Along the way, Sparrow must also contend with Will Turner and Elizabeth Swann, who are trying to rescue him from Jones’ clutches.

The film’s success was largely due to its beloved characters, thrilling action sequences, and captivating storyline. It also received positive reviews from critics, who praised its visual effects, performances, and direction.

Overall, “Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man’s Chest” remains one of the highest-grossing films of all time, cementing its place as a beloved classic of the fantasy and adventure genres.
